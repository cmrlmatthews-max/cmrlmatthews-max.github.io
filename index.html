03 — About

Design as a
dialogue with context

From the design studios of Louisiana Tech to commercial towers across Atlanta, my career has been built on equal parts rigorous training and real-world craft. I hold a B.S. in Architectural Studies and a Master of Architecture — both from Louisiana Tech University — and have spent the years since learning what buildings actually demand of the people who make them.

My work spans residential renovation and large-scale commercial. From managing hands-on projects at MasterBuilders Design Build to coordinating curtain wall details and consultant teams on high-rise mixed-use developments at Nelson Worldwide, I bring the same precision and care to every scale of work.
